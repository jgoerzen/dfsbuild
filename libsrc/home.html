Welcome to DFS

Press Esc to access the menus. The Help menu contains information on key bindings. Here is a quick list:
  • Up and down arrows, PgUp, PgDn scroll
  • Enter follows links
  • Left arrow goes back
  • "q" to quit
  • "g" to go to a url (prompts you for URL)
And now, press Enter to read the DFS documentation.
